# CFA Institute Research Challenge - 2nd Place (Top 2 of 18 Teams)

This repository contains the complete institutional-grade Equity Research Report and final presentation slides submitted for the **CFA Institute Research Challenge (2021–2022 Season)** in Casablanca, Morocco. 

Serving as an **Equity Research Analyst** and Team Leader, I managed a team of 5 analysts representing our institution. Our team achieved **2nd Place** out of 18 competing teams from leading Moroccan business schools.

## 📈 Project Scope & Methodologies
The competition required an exhaustive, professional-grade equity analysis and valuation of a publicly traded target company listed on the Casablanca Stock Exchange.

The finalized deliverables included:
* **Macroeconomic & Industry Analysis:** Deep-dive analysis of industry drivers, competitive positioning (Porter’s Five Forces), and macroeconomic headwinds/potentials.
* **Financial Analysis & Forensic Accounting:** Historical financial statements analysis, ratio analysis, and normalization of earnings.
* **Valuation Frameworks:** Developed robust quantitative valuation models using Discounted Cash Flow ($DCF$) models, Dividend Discount Models ($DDM$), and trading multiples ($P/E$, $EV/EBITDA$).
* **Risk Assessment:** Built a detailed investment risk matrix spanning operational, financial, market, and regulatory risks.
* **ESG Integration:** Systematic evaluation of Environmental, Social, and Governance criteria integrated directly into the valuation risk-premium calculation.

## 📁 Repository Deliverables
* `/report`: The comprehensive, professional written investment report (`CFA_Research_Challenge_Equity_Report.pdf`).
* `/presentation`: The deck used to defend our investment thesis in front of an expert panel of asset managers and CFA charterholders.
